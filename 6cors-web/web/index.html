    ABOUT US

    Born with the aim to bring out the best in every individual, the concept of IITG MUN is guided by a set of values and goals that seeks to provide every individual “a hands down” idea of the intricacies of the decision making process at international level, in an effort to provide holistic development of society as a unit. We have placed our firm belief on the idea of bringing together people from all over India, separated by regions, principles and cultures, united by humanity, to be a part of this platform and facilitate meaningful dialogue and discussion on wide ranging international issues, which affect the international community as a whole. With a vision to witness one of the most advanced, intense and crucial debate in the committees of IITG MUN, we strive to imbibe the virtues of leadership, co-operation, negotiations and diplomatic competence, and not only serve as a pathway to self-development, but also to nurture the policy makers of future generation, who will ultimately bring end to the misery and misfortunes of the world, through novel ideas and suggestions. We seek to break free from all the tangled threads of predefined notions and ideas, to engage in an enhancing debate and redefine all the boundaries and ideas, to provide all the students involved, from the delegates, the Executive Board, and the Secretariat; an idea of conflict resolution through peaceful means. At IITG MUN, you will definitely get the best you can, from the perfectly planned agendas, accomplished executive board, to most comprehensive delegate guides; to garner maximum participation of the delegates. We are at constant efforts to outdo ourselves to provide our delegates the best possible environment to make the MUN one of the most renowned one’s throughout India.
