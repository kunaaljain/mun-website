    Secretary General's Message

    Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,
    It is with utmost pleasure and honour that I welcome you to the fourth edition of the IIT-Guwahati Model United Nations Conference. From Friday, September 4th to Sunday, September 6th 2015, delegates from every nook and corner of the country will be convening in Guwahati, Assam for another round of refreshing debate and impassioned pleas by representatives. Hosted by the literary society of IIT Guwahati, the Fall edition promises to surpass the standards of excellence established by its preceding session.

    After an immensely successful third session, I, along with an extremely passionate Secretariat, am delighted to bring forth the legacy that we are trying to imbibe in IIT-Guwahati MUN. We want to focus on innovations in MUN-ing and enhance the overall delegate experience. We are excited to present three distinct committees with the most exceptional crisis committees, all of which will test delegates’ abilities to address and expand their global-view. Our exceptionally talented chairs and staff will steer the delegates through their exploration of various range of issues. This year’s conference aims at creating an entirely new IIT-Guwahati MUN experience, which begins before the delegates step into the conference.

    Our vision is to create a platform for the future leaders of the world to grace and shine upon! We at IIT-Guwahati MUN very firmly believe that the MUN Conferences are not just an exploration of world-views but also a platform to formulate one’s perception and ideology. Part of what makes this experience unforgettable are the socials and press- conferences organised to enhance the informal delegate interactions. To complete the delegate experience, we have 4 Pro-Nites during Techniche to provide the delegates with an opportunity to relax and simmer down.

    The Secretariat is hard at work planning an invaluable experience and an unforgettable conference. We want to watch IIT-Guwahati MUN prosper and we are committed to preserving the legacy of the excellent conference we had in the previous edition. At the same time we are equally excited to implement some new and exciting ideas as well as increase the number of councils. As we strive to ensure satisfaction, we would love to hear your concerns or comments. If you have any questions or concerns at any point, please don't hesitate to contact me by email - aditya.jain3645@gmail.com. I look forward to welcoming you in the Fall edition of IIT-Guwahati Model United Nations, Techniche 2015.

    Best Regards,
    Aditya Jain
    Secretary General
