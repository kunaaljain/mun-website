Meet our team

Aditya's fascination with the world of diplomatic simulations began around the time he was a student in Delhi. Back at home, where he is currently pursuing a B. Tech. in Chemical Science and Technology, he rediscovered his interest on being appointed as the Deputy Secretary General for the inaugural edition of the IITGMUN, Techniche and since then has been a frontrunner for the cause for MUNing. One year and innumerable interactions with MUNers nationwide, he now serves as the Secretary General. His interest in diplomacy extends beyond academic simulations as an active participant in foreign policy outreach programs like the Youth Forum for Foreign Policy and Indo-Myanmar . If you are beginning to think he has nothing in common with what one would normally associate with an engineering undergraduate, you would be right on many fronts. But you'd be better advised to wait till you meet him (and watch his attempts at being funny fall flat on your face) and decide for yourself.

Anybody can pass a quiz about Milind, because answering 'Manchester United' on all the questions would breeze you past the 40pc mark. Such is his devotion to the red devils, and to football in general. He is of the rare breed that finds the intricacies inherent in the complex structures of life equally fascinating as the simple laws of nature. Put more simply, he is a Physics undergraduate with a soft corner for biology. When he is not diving into the microstructures of life, he is the Deputy Secretary General of the IIT Guwahati Model United Nations Conference - working towards providing a new blend of experiences to incoming delegates. Or, you know, playing football. Or playing the guitar. Or making jokes with really bad puns in them. Not a man of many words, he is a joy to be with - unless you purposefully annoy him, which is one of our favorite pastimes. Nevertheless, when it comes to MUN, he tolerates no laxity. Rest assured delegate, the conference is in safe hands!

He's a Gujju who hails from Madhya Pradesh; currently pursuing graduation in Engineering Physics from an institute he got via JEE as he was too lazy to sign up for anything else; cynic, critic and general madman who derives most of his beliefs and philosophies (and also a love for stargazing) from Carl Sagan; has an unholy obsession with pop culture (and Indian Ocean music) that manifests in him making so many haywire references that most of what he talks or writes is gibberish; has been in a love-hate relationship with International Press since he found himself sitting in an MUN conference two years back with a 'Journalist' tag round his neck. In short, he's a mess.

From being uninitiated to the world of diplomacy a couple years back to being the frontrunner of the IIT Guwahati MUN as the Director General, Aditi sure has come a long way. Being an Inter IIT basketball player, she is quite good at juggling her time between her B. Tech. in Chemical Science and Technology, her forays into the world of MUN, basketball and getting rest of the members of the team to stop taking jibes at her!
