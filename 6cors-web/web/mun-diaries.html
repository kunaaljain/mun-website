MUN Diaries

The human experience is defined by stories. We are all in search of stories. In this search, some become vagabonds and some take the classier route of Model UN! Veterans and newbies alike, MUNs have given countless memories, anecdotes and experiences to thousands. They are there, taking up the prized corner of your heart. We, at the IITGMUN, have embarked on a quest of our own - to collect those memories into the cauldron we call ‪#‎MUNDiaries‬ and brew a potion for generations of delegates to relish. It is here that we need your help - pour out those corners of your heart into a small video and send it across! You can talk about anything - from a friendship across the border that MUNs strengthened to the social campaign you started from what you learnt - anything that motivates young minds to take a peek into the world of MUNing.
Looking forward to hear from you!
