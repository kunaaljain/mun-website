If one looks back at the military history of the past half century, one begins to see an unnerving trend. While nation-states have long been parties of major wars, the military power behind the trampling of complete cities in the Afghan, Somalian and Iraq wars; and the one behind many UN peacekeeping missions is not a nation-state at all. As you might have guessed by now, we are talking about private military corporations here. 'Guns for hire' would be an oversimplification, since they are unlike any mercenaries history has witnessed. The scenario becomes way more terrifying once one considers the business the PMCs are involved in is the business of war itself.
